Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Remedy, the ultimate platform dedicated to helping aspiring and professional models build successful careers. Our mission is to provide the tools, resources, and connections needed to turn your modeling dreams into reality.

How do I create a profile on your platform?

Simply sign up with your email, choose a plan (Free, Basic, or Premium), and complete your profile setup to start showcasing your portfolio.

How can I showcase my portfolio to potential clients?

Simply upload your best work to your profile, and potential clients can view and contact you directly through the platform.

Are the resources and guides suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our resources cater to all experience levels, from beginners to professionals.

Is there a refund policy if I’m not satisfied with a paid plan?

Yes, we offer a 14-day refund policy for all paid plans. Just reach out to our support team for assistance.
